Welcome to the SJC Sandbox Gaming Club!
Founded in 2016 at the St. John's College Santa Fe campus.

We host sandbox game servers for the benefit of St. John's students.
Currently we offer both a vanilla Minecraft server, and a FTB modded Minecraft server.
Join us for our official Spawn Building event on the vanilla server on February 26th, at 6:00 PM!

Powerful Servers

Our Minecraft servers are run courtesy of Bisect Hosting, with plenty of RAM and low latency.

Handy Admins

Our admins are knowledgeable about and active on the games. Contact them by email or in-game for help with griefing, cheating, glitches, advice, and paper topics!

Online 24/7

Never worry about having to ask us to turn the server on - you can play at any odd-hours you wish.

Secure Servers

Our servers are well secured, so that both your work and your privacy can be properly maintained.
